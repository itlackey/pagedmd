# The Cursed Catacombs

*A D&D 5e Adventure for 3rd-5th Level Characters*

## Adventure Synopsis

The quiet village of Thornhaven has been plagued by strange occurrences: crops withering overnight, livestock disappearing, and villagers reporting nightmares of a voice calling from beneath the earth. The party is hired to investigate the old catacombs beneath the village chapel, long sealed after a terrible plague two centuries ago.

What they discover is far worse than restless deadâ€”an ancient cult has begun to stir, and their dark ritual threatens to unleash something that should have stayed buried.

## Adventure Hooks

### The Desperate Priest

Father Aldric approaches the party in desperation. His congregation is terrified, and he suspects the old catacombs have been breached. He offers 100 gold pieces and the church's blessing to anyone willing to investigate.

### Family Ties

One character receives a letter from a relative in Thornhaven, begging for help. Strange dreams have driven the character's grandmother to the brink of madness, and the village healers are powerless to help.

### Bounty Notice

A notice on the tavern board offers 200 gold pieces for anyone who can stop the "disturbances" in Thornhaven. The notice is signed by the village council and has been posted for three weeks with no takers.

## Important NPCs

### Father Aldric Whitestone

*Human priest, age 54, Lawful Good*

- **Personality:** Earnest but frightened; wants to protect his flock
- **Goals:** Stop the nightmares plaguing his congregation
- **Secret:** Knows the catacombs were sealed for a reason but doesn't know what lies below
- **Voice:** Speaks softly, wrings hands when nervous

**Quote:** *"The bones of the dead should rest in peace, not cry out in the night."*

### Elder Miriam

*Human elder, age 68, Neutral Good*

- **Personality:** Sharp-minded despite her age; suspicious of outsiders
- **Goals:** Protect the village's secrets
- **Secret:** Her grandfather was one of the original seal-makers; she has his journal
- **Voice:** Terse, to the point, expects to be obeyed

**Quote:** *"You young folk always think you know better. The seal was placed for a reason."*

### Thom the Blacksmith

*Dwarf blacksmith, age 142, Neutral*

- **Personality:** Practical, no-nonsense, values action over words
- **Goals:** Keep his forge running and his family safe
- **Secret:** Has been having the nightmares but refuses to admit it
- **Voice:** Gruff, uses smithing metaphors

**Quote:** *"Problems don't fix themselves. You either hammer them into shape or toss them back in the forge."*

## Adventure Structure

### Part 1: The Village (30 minutes)

The party arrives in Thornhaven and investigates the strange occurrences. They can interview NPCs, examine the chapel, and learn the history of the catacombs.

**Key Locations:**
- The Chapel (area 1)
- The Village Green (area 2)
- Thom's Smithy (area 3)
- Elder Miriam's Home (area 4)

### Part 2: The Descent (1-2 hours)

The party enters the catacombs through a breach in the chapel's foundation. They navigate the upper levels, facing undead guardians and discovering signs of recent cultist activity.

**Key Challenges:**
- Zombie ambush (encounter 1)
- Collapsing corridor trap (hazard 1)
- Sealed chamber puzzle (puzzle 1)

### Part 3: The Depths (2-3 hours)

The party descends deeper into the catacombs, confronting the cult and their dark ritual. The final confrontation takes place in an ancient temple where the cult leader attempts to summon a shadow demon.

**Climax:**
- Cult ritual interrupted
- Boss fight with cult leader and shadow demon
- Choice: destroy the temple or seal it again

## Rewards

### Experience

- Defeating all encounters: 3,000 XP (750 per character in a party of 4)
- Solving the sealed chamber puzzle: 400 XP (100 per character)
- Successfully stopping the ritual: 600 XP (150 per character)
- **Total:** 4,000 XP

### Treasure

- Father Aldric's payment: 100 gp (or 200 gp if the bounty is fulfilled)
- From cultists: 150 gp in mixed coins, 3 pieces of jet worth 50 gp each
- In the sealed chamber: *Potion of Greater Healing*, *+1 Longsword* (named "Dawnbringer"), *Spell Scroll of Dispel Magic*
- From cult leader: *Ring of Mind Shielding*, *Unholy symbol worth 75 gp*

### Story Rewards

- The village's gratitude (future safe haven)
- Father Aldric's blessing (advantage on death saves while in the region)
- Elder Miriam's journal (contains lore about the region's history)
- Reputation as heroes
