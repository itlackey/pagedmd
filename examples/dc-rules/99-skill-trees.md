#### Punishing Counter

1. **0 AP** When an enemy within reach rolls a tough choice or worse on a basic attack against you, you parry their attack and take no damage. If they roll a failure or worse, you may also immediately  **ROLL THE DIE!** to make a basic attack on them. This counterattack does not count as an action on your turn. You can use this ability only once per round.
1. **1 AP** When an enemy within reach rolls a tough choice or worse on a basic attack against you, you parry their attack and take no damage. You may also immediately **ROLL THE DIE!** to make a basic attack on them to knock them prone, push them into a nearby area, or move into an area nearby doing 1 damage with this option. Using this ability does not count as an action on your turn. You can use this ability only once per round.
1. **1 AP** When an enemy you can see attacks you with a ranged weapon and rolls a tough choice or worse on a basic attack against you, you dodge their attack and take no damage. If they roll a failure or worse, you may also immediately **ROLL THE DIE!** to make a ranged basic attack on them if you have a ranged weapon and ammo available (you immediately stow whatever is in your hand(s) safely on your person). This counterattack does not count as an action on your turn. You can use this ability only once per round and the weapon you drew remains in your hand.

#### Pain Compliance

1. **1 AP** You use strength and technique to overpower a commoner or minion within reach (you cannot use this ability on bosses). Describe how you overpower them. You put the target in a compromised position dealing 1 hit point of damage to them. - Am I living up to my ideal?
